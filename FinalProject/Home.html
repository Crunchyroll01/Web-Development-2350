Beyond the Label: Rethinking Schizophrenia

Schizophrenia is a chronic and severe mental disorder that affects how a person thinks, feels, and behaves. It is characterized by a range of symptoms, including hallucinations, delusions, disorganized thinking, and impaired social functioning.

Despite its prevalence, schizophrenia remains a misunderstood and stigmatized condition. It is often associated with negative stereotypes and misconceptions, leading to discrimination and social isolation for those affected.

This website aims to provide information regarding the Beyond the Label: Rethinking Schizophrenia seminar
