Welcome to Indiana Tech

Our seminar will be held at the prestigious Indiana Institute of Technology, located in the heart of downtown Indiana. The venue offers state-of-the-art facilities, comfortable seating, and a welcoming atmosphere for all attendees.
Our building is the Snyder Academic Center, and our auditorium will be the Multi-Flex Theater.

Address: 1600 E Washington Blvd, Fort Wayne, IN 46803

Indiana Tech is easily accessible by car and rideshare services.

Parking Information

Parking is available on campus for seminar attendees. Please follow the signs to the designated parking areas for visitors. We recommend arriving early to secure a spot, as parking can fill up quickly during events.

Venue Auditorium

The venue features a modern auditorium equipped with the latest audiovisual technology, ensuring an optimal experience for all attendees.
