Meet Our Speakers

Discover the visionaries shaping the future of human psychology and mental health.

Valeria Cifuentes

Valeria is a renowned psychiatrist in the field of human psychology and mental health, with over 20 years of experience in the industry. Her innovative approach to integrating holistic practices into clinical spaces has earned her success among the best.

Regina Tenedor

Regina is a leading LCSW(Licensed Clinical Social Worker) in the field of human psychology and mental health, with a focus on creating innovative solutions to help combat schizophrenia and its stigmatization. Her work has been instrumental in shaping policies that promote mental wellness, public support systems, and community health.

Jared Thompson

Jared is an experienced psychologist specializing in abnormal psychology and its role in shaping the future of mental health care for affected individuals. His research focuses on the intersection of cognitive science and therapeutic interventions focused on improving or developing coping strategies; Aiding the clients in developing healthier ways to manage their conditions.
